What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn.

AI is used in healthcare, finance, transportation, and more. From smart assistants like Siri to self-driving cars, AI is transforming industries.

The Future of AI

The future of AI holds promise, but also raises concerns about ethics, jobs, and control. Proper governance will be key.
