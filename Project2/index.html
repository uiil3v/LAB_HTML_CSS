Al-Hilal Saudi Club

Al-Hilal Saudi Football Club is a professional Saudi football club, founded in 1957. Based in the Saudi capital, Riyadh, the club is considered the number one team in Saudi Arabia in terms of the number of local championships, and the most Asian club to win continental championships of various titles, with a total of 70 championships at the local, regional, and continental levels.
